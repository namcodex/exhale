Why this exists

Not everyone has someone safe to talk to

Emotional support should be accessible and free, not locked behind cost or waiting lists.

An AI companion can help people feel less alone in the moment.

But real professionals are essential for deeper, ongoing care.

Exhale exists to bridge the space between self-help and real therapy.

"From I feel overwhelmed, to I understand myself, to I know where to get real help if I need it."

Trust & safety, made clear
